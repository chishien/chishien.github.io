FOR THE STUDENTS, BY THE STUDENTS

The NUS Industrial & Systems Engineering Club is a sub-club of NUS Engin Club which aims to foster stronger bonds and friendships amongst ISE undergraduate students.

Friendships

Welcome to a new chapter of your University life!
